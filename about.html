About Lava Java

Lava java

Transported to a good time whilst hearing a table nearby clink their glasses to a happy moment, Lava Java takes the audience on a journey, travelling through the streets and time to the nostalgic character of Alexandria in the 60’s. Friends meeting over tapas, creating moments that are moved by their static momentum to the place they see as part of their history ,LAVA JAVA is a place reminiscent of a simpler Alexandria, welcoming friends and family on the journey to a future of memories that will be encapsulated in time as special memories Lava Javas is considered Alexandria’s first-ever authentic Spanish Restaurant.
